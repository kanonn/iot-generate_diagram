方案一：坦诚且寻求协作（最推荐，显得大家是队友）
这种写法不仅告知了现状，还礼貌地请求对方利用他们的专业能力或权限来帮忙。

"I checked internally, but unfortunately, we don't have the required access permissions for Model1 and aren't sure who the current administrator is."

"Since you're working on the configuration, could you please check the network range from your end? If you need any logs or other info I can access, let me know."
